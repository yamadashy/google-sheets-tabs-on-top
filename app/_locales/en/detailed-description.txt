This extension enables smoother operation by displaying Google Sheets tabs at the top.
By placing tabs at the top, it realizes intuitive navigation and supports the improvement of work efficiency with simple yet effective functions.

The Goolge Sheets and other information will not be sent or disclosed to the developer or any third party.

The source code is also available, and you can build and install it yourself.
For more details, please visit:
https://github.com/yamadashy/google-sheets-tabs-on-top

After installation, please refresh the Google Sheets page.
